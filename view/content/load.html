Community Program

To garner a stronger sense of community join us in our program!

Community App Development Contest

Organise a competition where people in the community can submit concepts for mobile applications that cater to specific requirements. Encourage a sense of pride and community ownership by enlisting the help of local developers and students to create these apps.

Digital Learning Workshop

Provide digital literacy classes to assist community members especially seniors in learning how to use computers, the internet, and online communication tools.

Smart Community Gardens

Put in place a smart technology system that incorporates data analytics, automation, and sensors for community gardens. This project brings together people who are interested in gardening and technology, while simultaneously promoting sustainable practices.
